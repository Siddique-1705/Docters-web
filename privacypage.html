3. How We Protect Your Information

We employ industry-standard measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of your personal information. These measures include physical, administrative, and technical safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure of your data.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following cases:
